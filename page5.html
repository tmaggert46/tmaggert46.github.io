Character/Class Comparison

This page will allow you to compare the different characters and classes to see how closely aligned the estimated max stats are for the character compared to the max stats for the class. A score is given that is calculated by finding the average distance from each stat point to the y=x line, which would represent perfect alignment. Note that this format allows characters with low growth rates to closely align with classes with low stat totals. Be sure that you are comprehending the score as an alignment score, not simply how strong that character/class combination is.
